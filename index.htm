Welcome to Uttarakhand Community Houston

We are a vibrant community of families from Uttarakhand (the Land of Gods - ‡§¶‡•á‡§µ‡§≠‡•Ç‡§Æ‡§ø) living in the Greater Houston area. Our mission is to preserve our rich cultural heritage, celebrate our traditions, and create a home away from home for all Uttarakhandis in Houston.

Join us in celebrating the beautiful culture of both Garhwal and Kumaon regions through festivals, cultural programs, and community gatherings.

About Our Community

Founded in 2015, the Uttarakhand Community of Houston brings together over 150 families who share a common bond - our love for Uttarakhand and its rich cultural heritage.

Our Mission

  • Preserve and promote Uttarakhandi culture, traditions, and languages
  • Organize cultural events and festivals throughout the year
  • Support new families settling in Houston
  • Teach our children about their cultural roots
  • Foster community service and charitable initiatives
  • Build bridges with other Indian communities in Houston
