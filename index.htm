About Our Community

Founded in 2015, the Uttarakhand Community of Houston brings together families who share a common bond - our love for Uttarakhand and its rich cultural heritage. We represent all 13 districts of Uttarakhand, from the peaks of Uttarkashi to the plains of Udham Singh Nagar.

Our Mission

  • Preserve and promote Uttarakhandi culture, traditions, and languages (Garhwali & Kumaoni)
  • Organize cultural events and festivals throughout the year
  • Support new families settling in Houston
  • Teach our children about their cultural roots and heritage
  • Foster community service and charitable initiatives
  • Build bridges with other Indian communities in Houston
  • Create a support network for personal and professional growth

Join Our Community

Become Part of Our Family!

We welcome all families from Uttarakhand or those who love Uttarakhandi culture to join our community. Being part of our community means:

  • Celebrating festivals and traditions together
  • Children learning about their cultural heritage
  • Making lifelong friendships with similar backgrounds
  • Supporting each other in a new country
  • Participating in cultural programs and events
  • Keeping our languages and traditions alive
  • Access to community WhatsApp group for updates
  • Opportunities to showcase talents and skills
